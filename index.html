Privacy Policy & GDPR Terms

Data Protection & Privacy

At Hablas, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with GDPR requirements.

What data we collect:

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Learning progress and statistics
  • Language preferences
  • Audio recordings for pronunciation practice (processed and deleted after analysis)

How we use your data:

  • To provide personalized language learning experience
  • To track your learning progress
  • To improve our services and algorithms
  • For essential service communications only

Your rights:

  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • You can request a copy of your data
  • You can opt-out of data collection (with limited functionality)
  • You have the right to data portability

Data Storage & Security:

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Audio recordings are processed for pronunciation analysis and then immediately deleted. We retain learning progress data to maintain your account functionality.

Contact:

For any privacy concerns or to exercise your GDPR rights, contact us through the settings menu or email us directly.

Key Promise: Your privacy matters to us. We will never sell your personal information and you maintain full control over your data at all times.
